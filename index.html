Project Overview

Our innovative system revolutionizes athletic training by integrating cutting-edge technologies to provide comprehensive performance analysis and enhancement tools for weightlifting athletes.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Biomechanical Feedback: Real-time analysis of lifting form and technique
  • 3D Posture Correction: Visual feedback for optimal body positioning
  • Fatigue Detection: Intelligent monitoring of athlete fatigue levels
  • Injury Prediction: Advanced algorithms to identify potential injury risks
  • Personalized Meal Planning: Nutrition optimization based on training load
  • Rehabilitation Tracking: Progress monitoring for injury recovery

All these features are seamlessly integrated into a mobile-friendly platform, allowing athletes and coaches to access critical performance data anytime, anywhere.
